Why this room exists.

On the Wall is a single-page fellowship exhibition built to practice laying out a page the way a gallery lays out a wall — sections that hold together, frames that sit where you put them, and enough breathing room between the two.

The Artists

5 files
Leonardo da Vinci
1452 – 1519

Italian Renaissance painter, engineer, and anatomist — the Mona Lisa took him over a decade to consider finished.

Vincent van Gogh
1853 – 1890

Dutch post-impressionist who painted The Starry Night from the window of the asylum where he was staying.

Edvard Munch
1863 – 1944

Norwegian expressionist who made several versions of The Scream, describing an anxious walk that inspired it.

Johannes Vermeer
1632 – 1675

Dutch Golden Age painter known for light and quiet domestic scenes — little else is documented about his life.

Pablo Picasso
1881 – 1973

Spanish artist and co-founder of Cubism who painted Guernica in a matter of weeks as a direct political response.
